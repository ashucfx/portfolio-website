Multi‑Vendor Infrastructure Delivery

Infrastructure 3 months 4+ vendors + exec stakeholders

Problem Complexity

Conflicting vendor timelines, no single owner, and leadership expectations shifting weekly. High exposure to scope creep and missed launch windows.

What I Drove

  • Dependency map and critical path for all vendor workstreams
  • Weekly alignment cadence with shared success criteria
  • Change control process tied to timeline impact

Who I Worked With

Vendor leads across infrastructure, security, and data; client CTO and operations leadership.

Execution Ownership

Owned delivery timeline, risk visibility, and stakeholder communication for the entire program.

Business Result

Delivered on time and within scope with a reusable coordination model adopted for future infrastructure initiatives.
